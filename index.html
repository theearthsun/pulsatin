Deep Groove Cleaning Instructions

  1. Preparation: Remove surface dust with a carbon fiber brush.
  2. Pre-Clean: Apply PULSATIN PRE CLEAN (avoid the center label).
  3. The Scrub: Use the goats hair brush following the grooves in full circles.
  4. Activation: Let the fluid sit for 8 minutes to dissolve the silence.
  5. The Rinse: Use distilled water and a clean brush to clear the path.
